Refresh DC August Meetup

We’ll be back at the National Digital Library Learning Center (located in the first floor of the Madison Building - through the front doors and straight ahead) for August’s meeting.

This month’s presentation will be brought to us by Jackson Wilkinson of Grassroots Enterprise. He’ll be presenting on Microformats, a hot (and sometime divisive) new means of tagging information in webpages.

If you have any questions, comments, or tips regarding Microformats, post them on our forum and we’ll address them in the presentation.

The closest Metro stop is Capitol South and you can find an excellent area map here.
