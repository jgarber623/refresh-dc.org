Wayan Vota, of One Laptop Per Child News, will be leading a discussion on the impact of OLPC on web design, the “average user experience,” content generation, and consumption itself in the developing world. With 5-10 million laptops set to be distributed in the next 12-18 months (the single largest computer monoculture ever shipped), children in the developing world will be consuming and producing educational content on a grand scale.
