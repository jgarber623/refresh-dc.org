Refresh DC February Meetup

Martin Ringlein, co-founder of nclud, will discuss the transition from side-work to serious-work as a freelancer, touching on everything from contracts and invoicing to getting clients and growing a portfolio. He will also talk about how to start freelancing, get more clients, and obtain larger projects.
